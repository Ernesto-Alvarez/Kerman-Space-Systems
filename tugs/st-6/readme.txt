ST-6 space station tug, double ended.

Short range. Tug should be assigned to a space station and have a clamp-o-tron jr port available at all times.
	* RCS only
	* Battery operation only
	* Short range communications

Designed for hauling cargo around the station, retrieving containers and maneuvering and docking tankers. Capable of docking to cargo and returning to the station without releasing.
