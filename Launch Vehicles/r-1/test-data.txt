Payload specification
Test mass: Mk1 capsule w/Stayputnik mounted axially and 2 radial reflectron DP-10. 880 Kg mass.
Extended test mass: Mk1 capsule with Mk0 tank, then Stayputnik mounted axially and and 2 radial reflectron DP-10. 1285 Kg mass.

Flight regimes
Normal:
	Gravity turn at 50m/s to 5 degrees
	Separation at 70 KM altitude
	Full thrust until propellant exhaustion
	No other control inputs after gravity turn

Vertical:
	Normal flight regime, except
	No gravity turn

Retrograde:
	Normal flight regime, except
	Gravity turn is retrograde, towards mountains west of KSC, GT angle 2.5 degrees

Shallow:
	Normal flight regime, except
	Gravity turn angle: 20 degrees
	MECO at 98% heat
